Project Horizon

Project Horizon is a complete redesign of Claude AI, driven by the goal of vastly improving the user experience. By addressing the challenges and limitations of the original interface, this project transforms Claude into a more intuitive and seamless AI platform, bridging the digital and physical worlds like never before.

EcoSphere

EcoSphere is a next-generation music streaming platform, designed to solve the common issues of existing apps. By enhancing user interfaces, expanding music libraries, and leveraging AI-driven personalization, EcoSphere offers a seamless and intuitive listening experience. It redefines how users discover, share, and enjoy music, setting a new standard in digital streaming.
