Imagine this: a promising new AI enters the scene, rivaling the legendary ChatGPT. I was captivated when Claude AI first launched, eager to see what it could do. The initial interactions? Exciting and full of potential! But as the honeymoon phase ended, the shine started to wear off.

My biggest frustration? The conversation length limit. Just as we were getting into a groove, bam! I’d hit the ceiling after five or so messages, forcing me to start a new thread. And it didn’t stop there. The daily limit for free users was just as aggravating—seven messages in, and I was locked out for hours, unless I opted for the pro version.

Now, I get it—businesses need to make money. But a poor user experience before I even consider paying? That’s a tough sell. It felt like I was being pushed away, not drawn in. The whole process was a barrier, not an invitation to upgrade.

"Why would I subscribe to a service that was already frustrating to use in its free version? A good user experience should draw me in, not push me away."

Enter Project Horizon. Our mission? To take Claude AI from frustrating to fabulous. We overhauled the entire platform, addressing every pain point.
